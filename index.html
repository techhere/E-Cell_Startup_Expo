About Us

E-Cell MANIT is a non-profit organization run by students working towards creating a culture of entrepreneurship among the students, awakening and encouraging the entrepreneurial spirit.The prime objective of E-Cell is to help the students to develop business ideas especially those who are eager to become entrepreneurs but are unsure about the way forward.

StartUp Expo

StartupExpo is a startups exhibition event, welcoming the top-notch Startups, Investors, Speakers, Organizations and attendees from all over India. The event will provide an amazing platform for startups to showcase their innovative products with the rest of the people thereby connecting them to an amazing community to help and propel their growth.
